Personal data protection

Privacy policy

The personal data you are asked to provide on the Copernicus land portal, with the exception of the ‘Global’ section, which is managed by the Joint Research Centre of the European Commission, will be processed by the European Environment Agency (EEA) and its contractors acting on behalf of the EEA in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 2018/1725 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 23 October 2018 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data by the Union institutions, bodies, offices and agencies and on the free movement of such data.

Processing of personal data

Purpose(s) of the processing:

  • Your personal data is collected and further processed for the following purposes:
    • Downloading the data provided on the Copernicus land portal
    • Responding to your individual requests
    • Informing you about portal updates and important events (only in case you have explicitly requested to receive such information)
    • Your registration to events organized or co-organized by the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service, where an on-line registration is available
    • Reporting on the use of the products of the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service available on the Copernicus land portal (only in an aggregated form) to the European Commission
  • Your personal data will not be redistributed by any means, nor used for any other purpose than those listed herein.

Recipients of the data processed:

  • For the purposes detailed above, access to your personal data is given to EEA staff members in charge of managing the Copernicus land portal as well as to entities acting under contract with the EEA, and identified as processors in the context of the data protection legislation
  • In addition, in case of an on-line registration to events where the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service is acting as a co-organizer, access to your personal information might be provided to other co-organizing institution(s)

Categories of data processed:

  • The personal data collected and further processed is the information you encode yourself in the online registration to the Copernicus land portal, to the events, for which an on-line registration is open, and to the Copernicus service desk contact form
  • The compulsory fields for your on-line registration to the Copernicus land portal concern your name, e-mail address, institutional domain and professional thematic domain
  • The compulsory fields for your on-line registration to events are name, e-mail address, organization, position, city and country. Where the event is co-organized by the European Commission, an additional personal information required to provide is date of birth, nationality, ID document number and ID document validity date for the purpose of getting access to the venue
  • Only your e-mail address is compulsory in the Copernicus service desk contact form

Modalities for the processing operation:

  • Personal data is provided via submission forms available online. When submitting data online, it will be an automated processing operation whereby you input the data directly in the form, which is then stored in the respective database

Right of access and rectification:

You have no direct access to the data stored. If you wish to modify or delete any of your personal data (including deleting your account on the Copernicus land portal), you should address your request to the Copernicus service desk at our contact form.

Legal basis:

  • The legal basis for processing your personal data is the performance of the tasks delegated by the European Commission to the EEA for the implementation of the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service of the Copernicus programme

Data retention:

  • Your personal data concerning the registration to the Copernicus land portal will be kept during the period of performance of the tasks delegated by the European Commission to the EEA for the implementation of the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service of the Copernicus programme unless you explicitly request to delete your data (see above)
  • Your personal data concerning the on-line registration to the events will be kept during the period of performance of the tasks delegated by the European Commission to the EEA for the implementation of the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service of the Copernicus programme, if you actively request this during your registration to the event, otherwise it will be deleted 6 months after the respective event has taken place
  • Your personal data concerning your requests to the Copernicus service desk contact form will be kept during the period of performance of the tasks delegated by the European Commission to the EEA for the implementation of the Copernicus Land Monitoring Service of the Copernicus programme

Right to appeal:

  • You are entitled to have recourse at any time to the European Data Protection Supervisor (http://www.edps.europa.eu; EDPS@edps.europa.eu) if you consider that your rights under Regulation(EC) No 2018/1725 have been infringed as a result of the processing of your personal data by the EEA
  • You may also contact the EEA’s Data protection Officer (DPO) in case of any difficulties relating to the processing of your data at the following e-mail address: dpo@eea.europa.eu.
